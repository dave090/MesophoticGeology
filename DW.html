Deep reef communities (30-150 m) called mesophotic  coral ecosystems  (MCEs) have recently captured the attention of the scientific community, particularly as a result of both the continual global degradation of shallow water reefs and the development of the mesophotic refugia hypothesis. While recent technological advances in SCUBA have permitted increased research into MCE biology and ecology, there is still a critical gap in knowledge of the geology of mesophotic reefs.         

    This website is dedicated to research conducted by the mesophotic coral reef geology laboratory group, led by Dr. David K. Weinstein.  It is also to provide contact information and news from group members.  More content can be found on our facebook page.  Please feel free to contact us with any questions, comments, or feedback.
